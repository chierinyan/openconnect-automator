## Clash Routed OpenConnect

> Automaticly maintains an [OpenConnect](https://www.infradead.org/openconnect/) connection that
> requires mail 2FA, and provide routing by [clash](https://dreamacro.github.io/clash/advanced-usages/openconnect.html)


Please follow the regulations of your VPN provider and use at your own risk.


### Notes

* The POP service of the email should be enabled
